# Common Research Areas by Topic

Below are suggested research areas for popular topics that can help structure your research prompt:

## Technology Evaluation

1. **Technical Capabilities & Features**
   - Core functionality
   - Performance metrics
   - Scalability characteristics
   - Security features

2. **Implementation Considerations**
   - Integration requirements
   - Development complexity
   - Deployment options
   - Maintenance needs

3. **Ecosystem & Community**
   - Community size and activity
   - Available resources and documentation
   - Third-party integrations
   - Long-term support outlook

4. **Cost Structure**
   - Licensing models
   - Operational costs
   - Hidden costs
   - ROI analysis

5. **Market Position**
   - Vendor stability
   - Market share trends
   - Competitive advantages
   - Future roadmap

## Market Research

1. **Market Dynamics**
   - Size and growth rate
   - Segmentation
   - Regional variations
   - Seasonal patterns

2. **Customer Analysis**
   - Demographics
   - Psychographics
   - Needs and pain points
   - Decision-making process

3. **Competitive Landscape**
   - Direct competitors
   - Indirect competitors
   - Positioning strategies
   - Competitive advantages

4. **Trends & Disruptions**
   - Emerging technologies
   - Changing regulations
   - Shifting consumer preferences
   - Macroeconomic factors

5. **Go-to-Market Strategy**
   - Pricing models
   - Distribution channels
   - Marketing approaches
   - Partnership opportunities
